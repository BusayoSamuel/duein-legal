Common questions

No departures showing?

  • Check you have an internet connection.
  • Tap Refresh now on the selected stop.
  • On first launch, wait for timetable data to finish downloading, or tap continue without timetables — live departures still work for many stops.

Times look wrong?

  • Live times come from the National Transport Authority feed and can change when buses are delayed or cancelled.
  • Try refreshing — times are updated when you refresh the board.

Walk / drive times missing?

  • Allow location permission when prompted (optional).
  • You can still use departures without location.

Widget not working?

  • Long-press the home screen → add the DueIn widget → pick your stop.
  • Tap the widget to open the app at that stop.
